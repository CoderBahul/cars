IR Sensor Car Safety System

The IR sensor detects obstacles in the car's path. If an obstacle is detected, the car stops, an alarm beeps, and a sad face appears as a signal that the car cannot move further.

Obstacle Detected!

When an IR sensor detects an object in the path of the car, it triggers the following actions:

  • The car stops moving immediately to avoid a collision.
  • An alarm goes off to alert the driver and passengers.
  • A sad face emoji is displayed, indicating that the car is stuck due to the obstacle.
