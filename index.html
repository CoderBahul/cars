RC Car Specifications

  • Obstacle Detection: Stops the car when an obstacle is detected.

Key Features

  • Black Line Following: The car follows a predefined black line path.
  • Remote Control: Full control through the app for forward, reverse, and turns.
  • Alcohol Detection: Integrated alcohol sensor that detects presence of alcohol fumes.
  • Obstacle Detection: Using ultrasonic sensors, the car stops when obstacles are detected.
  • Day/Night Light Sensor: Automatically turns on headlights in the dark.
